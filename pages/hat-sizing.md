To measure your headsize roll a tape measure around your head just above your ears - this is where the hat sits. Leave a finger's-width of space for comfort. Sizes are preferably in CM. 

MCK Millinery is happy to consult on custom sizing. We can also make small adjustments in our standard sizes without additional costs.
